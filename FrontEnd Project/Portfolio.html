About Me

Hello! I'm Rishikesh Yogesh Boril a Computer Engineering student currently in my 4th year (BE), passionate about Python for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. My journey in technology has been marked by consistent academic growth, with my CGPA improving from 5.84 in first year to 8.71 in third year.

My domain expertise lies in Python for Data Science, particularly in AI applications. I've completed an internship at YBI Foundation in "AIML with Data Science" where I worked on image classification projects using TensorFlow.

Beyond academics, I'm actively involved in research, having participated in the First International Conference on "Applications of AI in Engineering" (ICAAIE-25) with a paper on "Automated Retinal Analysis Using Deep Convolutional Networks."

I believe in giving back to society through volunteering activities, including blood donation and community service during religious festivals.

My Goals:

  • Advance my expertise in AI and Machine Learning
  • Contribute to meaningful research in healthcare AI
  • Build innovative solutions that impact society positively
  • Continue learning and growing in the field of Data Science
